Bayesian inference

The usual place to start with Bayesian approaches is the wonderful text book Statistical Rethinking Richard McElreath:

  • xcelab.net/rm
  • pdf on github.com/Booleans

For our purposes here a good start would be looking at Chapters 1 to 11 but skipping or just skimming Chapters 5 to 7.

Richard McElreath, who wrote the book has some nice youtube videos, the first is here:

  • youtube.com
or, for a new version, here:
  • youtube.com

A more classic textbook be leading people in the field is

  • sites.stat.columbia.edu/gelman/book
and this review, though it has its faults, is useful:
  • nature.com
